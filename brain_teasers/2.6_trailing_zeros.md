Trailing zeros
How many trailing zeros are there in 100! (factorial of 100)?

solution: 

Trailing zero number only depends on how many 10s we can make in 100! 

Who makes 10? 2*5, we have way more 2s then 5, so how many 5 is miltiplied in 100!, we will have that number of 0s.

How many 5 is multifplied in 100! 
single 5: 5,10, 15,20....100  = `[1 .. 20 ] * 5` -> that's 20 number, has one 5

when we count `[1 .. 20]` there are still 5, 10, 15, 20 -> that's 4numbers has 5

so in total it's 24 5 so there will be 24 zeros in the trail
